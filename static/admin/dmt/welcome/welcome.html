WELCOME!

The Dojo's Maintenance Tool (DMT for short) provides a set of tools for monitoring and maintaining your Dojo. MONITORING DOJO STATUS A dashboard for monitoring the health of some components of your Dojo. PUSHTX STATUS A dashboard for monitoring the transactions pushed through your Dojo. TOOLS PAIRING Pair your wallet to your Dojo by scanning a QRCode. XPUBS TOOL Everything you need to manage your XPUBs manually.
Check if a XPUB is tracked by your Dojo. Import and track a XPUB. Rescan the full history of a XPUB. ADDRESSES TOOL Everything you need to manage your addresses manually.
Check if an address is tracked by your Dojo. Import and track an address. Rescan the full history of an address. TRANSACTIONS TOOL Check if a transaction is found in a block or in the mempool of your full node. BLOCKS RESCAN Rescan the transactions confirmed by the blocks in a given range. HELP DOJO TELEGRAM CHAT Get support from the community for all things related to your Dojo (requires Telegram). WHIRLPOOL TELEGRAM CHAT Get support from the community for all things related to Whirlpool (requires Telegram). SW TELEGRAM CHAT Get support from the community for all things related to your Samourai Wallet (requires Telegram).
